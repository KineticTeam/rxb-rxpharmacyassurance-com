About RxPharmacy Assurance

RxPharmacy Assurance, Inc. is a Vermont domiciled Sponsored Captive managed by Marsh Captive Management. Upon meeting membership eligibility requirements, self-funded plans of all sizes can access affordable supplemental stop-loss insurance designed specifically to manage pharmacy risk within RxPharmacy Assurance. The unique solution limits the amount that a plan or its stop-loss carrier are required to pay to safeguard against unexpected and potentially catastrophic specialty claims.
